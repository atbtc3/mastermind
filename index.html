MASTERMIND

Rules

The robot picks a secret combination of 4 colors.

You have 10 tries to guess it.

Click on the circles to choose your colors, then press Go.

After each try, feedback dots appear:

Right color, right position
Right color, wrong position

You have 40 seconds per line. If time runs out, the line is submitted as is.
